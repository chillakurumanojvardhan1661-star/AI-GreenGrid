# AI GreenGrid – Intelligent Renewable Energy Management Platform

AI GreenGrid is a futuristic smart energy management platform designed to optimize renewable energy usage, improve power grid efficiency, and empower citizens to actively participate in sustainable energy consumption.

The platform provides two powerful interfaces: a **Department Command Center for energy authorities** and a **Citizen Energy Dashboard for consumers**, enabling intelligent monitoring, forecasting, and optimization of electricity distribution.

### Key Capabilities

**Interactive Energy Command Center**
The Admin portal functions as a real-time grid control dashboard where energy authorities can monitor power generation, renewable utilization, storage levels, and dynamic energy pricing. AI-powered forecasting predicts solar, wind, and hydro energy production, enabling smarter grid management.

**Digital Twin Power Grid Simulation**
AI GreenGrid includes a virtual **Digital Twin of the power grid**, allowing administrators to visualize energy flow across power plants, storage systems, and transmission lines. The platform can simulate different scenarios such as renewable surges or sudden demand spikes.

**Regional Energy Demand Heatmap**
A geographic heatmap provides insights into electricity demand across regions, helping operators quickly identify overloaded areas and optimize distribution.

**AI Grid Intelligence & Security**
The system detects anomalies such as abnormal consumption spikes, potential energy theft, and grid instability. Predictive maintenance algorithms also identify equipment risks before failures occur.

**Citizen Energy Dashboard**
The user portal empowers citizens with detailed electricity insights including real-time consumption tracking, usage predictions, sustainability scores, and personalized energy-saving recommendations.

**Energy Credit Marketplace**
Users earn energy credits for responsible energy consumption such as using electricity during renewable surplus periods. These credits can be traded through an integrated marketplace to reduce electricity bills.

**Carbon Footprint & Sustainability Tracker**
Citizens can monitor the environmental impact of their energy usage, including renewable contribution and CO₂ emission reductions.

**Advanced Energy Analytics**
The platform provides highly interactive analytics dashboards using dynamic charts, visual comparisons, and historical insights to help both authorities and citizens make data-driven decisions.

### Design & User Experience

AI GreenGrid features a **modern glassmorphism interface with smooth animations, glowing energy flow visualizations, and interactive dashboards**. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the system delivers a premium user experience similar to advanced smart grid control platforms used in modern energy infrastructure.
